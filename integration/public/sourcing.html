Match scoring

Every candidate, scored against your job description

Paste a job description or pull in a live job order. The AI extracts the must-haves and nice-to-haves, then scores and ranks every candidate 0-100, with a transparent breakdown and rationale.

How the score is built

Transparent, not a black box

  • Skills match, required vs. demonstrated, weighted by recency
  • Seniority & scope, has she operated at this level and ownership?
  • Domain & context, real-time / fintech / scale experience
  • Location & logistics, geo, visa, remote-readiness
  • Timing signal, is she reachable right now?

Every score expands into a sentence of reasoning, so you trust the ranking, and can defend it to a hiring manager.

The programmable grid

Enrich like a spreadsheet, with AI in every cell

Every candidate is a row. Every signal is a column. But unlike a spreadsheet, each cell can call an AI agent or a live API. Add a column, write a prompt, and watch the grid fill itself.

  • Verified emails & phones from 50+ providers behind one cell
  • "Why now" research summarized per person
  • Custom AI columns, comp estimate, likelihood to move, personalized hook
  • Run on every row in one click, or trigger per cell

Precision controls

ICP, disqualifiers & decision-maker discovery

Define exactly who counts, and who doesn't, so the list stays clean and the score stays honest.

Codify the traits of your best hires once. Every search and score is measured against it automatically.

Disqualifiers

Decision-maker discovery
