Runs itself every morning

The Daily Cadence

A seven-step automated workflow fires before you've finished your coffee. You only step in where human judgment creates the most value, the approval queue.

7:00

Signal pull

Scans the market for fresh buying signals, funding, hiring velocity, leadership changes, across every tracked account.

7:15

Scoring

Ranks every new signal and prospect against your ICP and disqualifiers, so only the highest-fit targets advance.

7:30

Enrichment

Resolves verified emails, phone numbers, and "why now" context from 50+ providers behind one cell.

7:45

LLM drafting

Writes personalized first-touch copy for every prospect, grounded in their real history and the triggering signal.

8:30

Approval queue

The one human step. Skim, tweak, approve, or let high-confidence drafts auto-send. You stay in control.

9:00

Channel push

Sends across email, LinkedIn, and SMS at safe daily caps, respecting domain warmup and account health.

All day

Reply processing

The AI classifies every response, replies to the simple ones, and escalates hot leads to you in real time.
