Questions, answered.

What happens after the 14-day trial?

Your workspace keeps everything you set up. Add a payment method to keep the Admin Portal active; nothing is deleted while you decide, and you pick up right where you left off.

Do you charge per seat?

No. Seats are unlimited on every plan. Pricing tracks your monthly sending volume and the optional per-unit services you enable (phone lookups, AI personalization).

Can I run it under my own brand?

Yes. The Scale plan is white-label ready: your logo, your brand name, your accent color, and your own domain, with recruiter portals that never mention us.

Does my ATS stay the source of truth?

Yes. RecruitersOS syncs replies, touches, and placements to your ATS (Loxo is the verified primary) rather than replacing it.

What if I need more than 20k emails a month?

That is exactly what the Scale conversation is for: we plan domains, warm-up, and deliverability with you so volume grows without burning your sender reputation.
