The recruiting motion

Signal → Shortlist → Engage → Place

Every placement follows the same path. The Recruiter OS runs all four steps in one workspace.

📡

Signal

A reorg, a layoff, a 4-year tenure mark, an "open to work" flag, the system tells you the moment a candidate becomes reachable.

🎯

Shortlist

People-GPT search builds the list; the JD match score ranks everyone against the actual job description.

💬

Engage

Personalized outreach across email, LinkedIn, and SMS, with AI carrying the conversation until they're ready to talk.

🏆

Place

Screening calls, notes, and stages sync straight to your ATS. Close the role and bank the fee.
