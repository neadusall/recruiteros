LinkedIn Engine

Turn connections into conversations.

Most recruiting still lives on LinkedIn, and most of it is wasted on generic invites that never get accepted. The LinkedIn Engine personalizes every touch, follows up the moment someone connects, and keeps your account safe while it scales.

Start on LinkedIn How it works
✕ The default

"I'd like to add you to my professional network."

Blank invite, no context. Accepted rarely, remembered never, and one careless tool away from a restricted account.

✓ The LinkedIn Engine

"Hi Anja, your work on the Trade Republic order flow is some of the cleanest trading UI I've seen. Building a small group of senior React folks I genuinely rate, would love to connect."

Written from her real profile. Sent at a human pace. Followed up the second she accepts.

The LinkedIn motion

Target → Connect → Warm up → Message → Convert

Every step is personalized, paced like a human, and pauses the instant someone replies.

1

Target

Pull people straight from a People-GPT search, a saved list, or a Sales Navigator import, already scored against your role.

2

Connect

AI writes a personalized invite from each profile. Optional profile-view and skill-endorsement warmups raise acceptance before the request even lands.

3

Warm up

The moment an invite is accepted, the follow-up fires automatically, no manual checking, no leads slipping through.

4

Message

A multi-step LinkedIn sequence, including text and voice notes, runs alongside email and SMS in the same campaign.

5

Convert

Replies hit one unified inbox, get classified by AI, and hot threads route to you with full context to book the call.

One LinkedIn inbox

Every reply, classified and queued

Connection acceptances, messages, and InMail replies land in the same Response inbox as your email and SMS, sorted by intent so you act on the warm ones first.

  • Auto-classified as positive, soft yes, timing, fit, or referral
  • Reply detection pauses the sequence so no one gets a robotic follow-up
  • Full context attached, the campaign, the role, and the whole thread
  • One-click handoff to a human the moment intent spikes

The LinkedIn toolset

Everything you need to run LinkedIn at scale

AI-personalized invites

Every connection request is written from the person's real profile, headline, posts, and shared context, not a template.

Accept-triggered follow-ups

The instant an invite is accepted, the next message sends automatically. No watching the inbox, no missed windows.

InMail & open profiles

Reach people you're not connected to with AI-drafted InMail, and message open profiles for free where available.

Sales Navigator import

Pull a Sales Navigator search or list straight into a campaign, enriched and scored on arrival.

LinkedIn voice notes

Auto-generate a short personal voice note for high-interest prospects, the touch almost no one else sends.

Profile-view & endorsement warmup

Visit profiles and endorse skills before the invite lands to lift acceptance rates organically.

Auto-withdraw stale invites

Pending requests are pulled back after a set window to protect your acceptance ratio and free up invite slots.

Account-safe sending

Human-like delays, working-hours windows, and per-account daily caps keep you well inside LinkedIn's limits.

Multi-account orchestration

Run several seats in parallel with smart rotation and live health monitoring on every account.

Omni-channel sequences

LinkedIn steps interleave with email and SMS in one campaign, so a prospect feels one coherent conversation.

Acceptance & reply analytics

See acceptance rate, reply rate, and meetings booked by message variant and by account.

ATS write-back

Connections, replies, and notes sync to your ATS automatically, so LinkedIn activity never lives in a silo.

Built to protect your account

Aggressive results, conservative behavior
