Tax Senior or Manager - West Palm Beach or Fort Lauderdale - Hybrid

📍 Palm Beach or Fort Lauderdale - Hybrid🕑 Hybrid💰 $90,000–$140,000🏷️ Accounting & Finance

Our public accounting client is looking for Senior Tax Accountants and Managers to work on their family office, real estate, and C-Corp clients.

  • Client Relationship Management: Serve as the primary point of contact for a portfolio of clients. Cultivate and strengthen relationships by providing exceptional service, anticipating needs, and delivering proactive tax planning and consulting advice.
  • Technical Tax Expertise: Oversee and review complex tax returns for various entity types, including:
    • Family Offices & High-Net-Worth Individuals (HNWI): Comprehensive planning and compliance for trusts, estates, gifts, and sophisticated individual returns (Forms 1040, 1041, 709).
    • Real Estate Entities: Partnership and S-corporation returns (Forms 1065, 1120-S) with expert knowledge of partnership tax allocations, basis calculations, passive activity limitations, and like-kind exchanges (IRC Section 1031).
    • Corporations: C-corporation compliance and planning (Form 1120), including consolidated returns and multi-state tax issues.
  • Tax Planning & Consulting: Identify and implement complex tax strategies to minimize client tax liabilities. Advise clients on structuring transactions, compliance changes, and other strategic initiatives.
  • Team Leadership & Development: Mentor, train, and develop senior and staff accountants. Manage workflow, ensure adherence to deadlines and quality standards, and foster a collaborative team environment.
