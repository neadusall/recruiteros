Clinical Director - ABA Company in Savannah GA

📍 Savannah GA💰 $90,000–$100,000🏷️ Healthcare/ABA

Our client has built from the ground up to be client inspired, parent guided, and clinically informed. They seeks a new Clinical Director to lead their Savannah GA facility.

How we value you:

  • Inclusive culture
  • Competitive compensation
  • Career progression
  • Benefits
  • Collaborative environment
  • Perks
  • Continuing education opportunities

Clinical Director, BCBA (Board Certified Behavior Analyst) Responsibilities include, but are not limited to:

  • You'll leverage internal and external resources and support of the performance of your role
  • You'll provide clinical oversight and guidance to the clinic's BCBAs
  • Manage clinic escalations
  • Meet a small case load requirement
  • Be present in the clinic 5 days a week working remotely on designated 'Flex Days'
  • You'll work closely with Co-Founders on leadership responsibilities and meeting KPIs
  • You'll hold bi-weekly 1:1 meetings (~15-30 minutes) with each BCBA in the clinic to offer support, coaching, oversight and feedback.
  • You'll host a bi-weekly team meeting to align on goals, address needs, and build connection.
  • Oversee the clinic's day to day
  • You'll send out a monthly clinic update to your team, sharing key wins, progress towards the clinic's goals, shoutouts, and reminders.
  • You will provide clinical oversight, including through parallel supervision, Learning Tree and Client Review meetings, and by upholding clinical standards.
  • Provide other job functions as may reasonably arise in the performance of ABA duties, as directed by company management.
