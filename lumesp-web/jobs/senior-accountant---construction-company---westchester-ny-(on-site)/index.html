Senior Accountant - Construction Company - Westchester NY (On-Site)

📍 Westchester, NY🕑 On-Site💰 $130,000–$150,000🏷️ Accounting & Finance

Our client is a $40M+ construction engineering and services supply company operating across the U.S. and Canada as a strategic, free-standing segment of a larger private family fund. With strong financial backing and ambitious plans for organic and acquisition-driven growth, the business is entering a major scaling phase. They are now seeking a Senior Accountant to support the Head of Finance and help strengthen the company's expanding accounting function.

Responsibilities

  • Lead month-end and year-end close activities, including journal entries, reconciliations, and analysis.
  • Maintain the general ledger and ensure accuracy across accounts.
  • Prepare and review balance sheet reconciliations, resolving discrepancies proactively.
  • Manage fixed asset accounting, including additions, disposals, depreciation.
  • Reconcile bank accounts, monitor cash activity, and manage foreign currency (forex) transactions, including revaluations and reporting of exchange gains/losses.
  • Perform intercompany reconciliations, eliminations, and settlement processes across multiple entities.
  • Support accounts payable processing and ensure compliance with internal controls.
  • Coordinate project and monthly billing, including use of Textura with the Project Management team.
  • Prepare budgets and forecasts, providing analysis and insight for management review.
  • Serve as a point of contact for external auditors: plan, prepare lead schedules, and provide support.
  • Ensure compliance with U.S. GAAP, internal controls, and company policies.
  • Identify and implement process improvements to increase efficiency and strengthen reporting.
