Privacy Policy

Last Updated: June 15, 2026

Introduction

Lume Search Partners (“Lume,” “we,” “us,” or “our”) respects your privacy and is committed to protecting your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and protect information through our website at lumesp.com, our forms and applications, our telephone and text-message (SMS) communications, and our related services (collectively, the “Platform”).

By accessing or using the Platform, or by providing your information to us, you agree to the practices described in this Privacy Policy. If you do not agree with any part of this Privacy Policy, please do not access or use the Platform or provide us with your information.

1. Information We Collect

We collect information that identifies you directly (“Personal Information”) and information that does not directly identify you (“Non-Personal Information”).

A. Information You Provide to Us

You may voluntarily provide Personal Information when you:

  • Submit a contact, inquiry, or “request a call” form
  • Apply for a job opportunity or submit your resume
  • Opt in to receive calls or text messages from us
  • Sign up for newsletters, alerts, or updates
  • Communicate with our team by phone, text, email, or web form

This may include your full name, email address, mobile or other telephone number, organization name and role, resume and professional profile data, and any other information you choose to share with us.

B. Information We Collect Automatically

When you visit or interact with the Platform, we may collect technical and usage information, including your IP address, browser type and device information, the pages you visit and how you interact with them, and information collected through cookies and similar tracking technologies.

C. Information From Third Parties

We may receive information about you from third-party sources such as analytics providers, professional networks, job boards, and references you provide, consistent with applicable law.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Provide, maintain, operate, and improve the Platform and our recruiting and advisory services
  • Respond to your inquiries, requests, and applications
  • Contact you by phone, email, and—where you have opted in—text message (SMS) regarding job opportunities, interview scheduling, application status, and related recruiting communications
  • Send you newsletters, alerts, and updates you have requested
  • Analyze usage trends and improve our website and communications
  • Protect the security and integrity of the Platform and comply with our legal obligations

3. SMS / Text Messaging and Mobile Information

If you provide us with your mobile phone number and opt in to receive text messages—for example, by submitting a form, application, or written request in which you consent to be contacted by SMS—we may send you recruiting-related text messages, such as opportunity alerts, interview scheduling, application follow-ups, and account or service updates.

Message frequency varies. Message and data rates may apply. You can opt out of text messages at any time by replying STOP to any message, and you can request help by replying HELP or by contacting us at info@lumesp.com. For full messaging terms, see our Terms of Use.

No mobile information will be shared with third parties or affiliates for marketing or promotional purposes. All of the categories described in the “How We Share Your Information” section below exclude text messaging originator opt-in data and consent; this information will not be shared with any third parties. We do not sell, rent, or share your mobile opt-in or SMS consent information.

4. How We Share Your Information

We may share your information only as described below:

  • Service Providers – vendors who help us operate the Platform and our services (for example, hosting, email and messaging delivery, and analytics providers), who are permitted to use the information only to perform services for us.
  • Prospective Employers – relevant portions of a candidate’s professional information, shared only with the candidate’s prior consent in connection with a specific opportunity.
  • Legal Authorities – when required by law, subpoena, or to protect our rights, safety, or property.
  • Successors – in connection with a merger, acquisition, financing, or sale of assets.

As stated above, text messaging originator opt-in data and SMS consent are never shared with any third parties or affiliates for marketing or promotional purposes. We do not sell your personal information.

5. Cookies and Tracking Technologies

We use cookies and similar technologies to operate the website, remember your preferences, and understand how the Platform is used. You can usually control cookies through your browser settings; disabling cookies may affect how the website functions.

6. Your Choices and Rights

You may, at any time:

  • Opt out of marketing emails by using the unsubscribe link in any email
  • Opt out of text messages by replying STOP
  • Request that we access, update, correct, or delete your Personal Information

Depending on where you live, you may have additional rights under applicable privacy laws (such as the California Consumer Privacy Act or similar state laws), including the right to know what Personal Information we hold about you and to request its deletion. To exercise any of these choices, contact us at info@lumesp.com. We will not discriminate against you for exercising your rights.

7. Data Retention

We retain Personal Information for as long as necessary to provide our services, maintain candidate and client relationships, and comply with our legal obligations, after which we securely delete or anonymize it.

8. Security

We implement reasonable administrative, technical, and physical safeguards designed to protect your information. However, no method of transmission over the Internet or electronic storage is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

9. Children’s Privacy

The Platform is intended for individuals 18 years of age and older and is not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect Personal Information from children under 13. If we learn that we have collected such information, we will take steps to delete it.

10. Third-Party Links

The Platform may contain links to third-party websites or services. We are not responsible for the privacy practices or content of those third parties, and we encourage you to review their privacy policies.

11. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The updated version will be posted on this page with a revised “Last Updated” date. Your continued use of the Platform after changes are posted constitutes acceptance of the updated Privacy Policy.
