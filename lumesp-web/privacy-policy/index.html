Introduction

Lume (“we,” “us,” or “our”) respects your privacy and is committed to protecting your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and protect information through our website, applications, and services (collectively, the “Platform”) provided by Lume Search Partners.

By accessing or using our Platform, you agree to the terms of this Privacy Policy. If you do not agree with any part of this Privacy Policy, do not access or use the Platform.

1. Information We Collect

We may collect information that identifies you directly (Personal Information) and information that does not directly identify you (Non-Personal Information):

A. Personal Information You Provide

You may voluntarily provide Personal Information when you:

  • Submit contact or inquiry forms
  • Apply for job opportunities
  • Sign up for newsletters or updates
  • Communicate with our team

Examples include:

  • Full Name
  • Email address
  • Phone number
  • Organization name/role
  • Resume or professional profile data

B. Information Collected Automatically

We may collect technical and usage information when you visit or interact with the Platform, including:

  • IP address
  • Browser type and device information
  • Pages visited and interaction data
  • Cookies and similar tracking technologies

C. Third-Party Information

We may receive data about you from third-party sources (e.g., analytics providers, social networks).

2. How We Use Your Information

We may use your information for purposes including, but not limited to:

  • Providing, maintaining, and improving our Platform
  • Responding to your inquiries and requests
  • Communicating updates, news, and job opportunities
  • Analyzing usage trends
  • Complying with legal obligations

3. How We Share Your Information

We may share your information with:

  • Service Providers – third parties who assist in operating the Platform
  • Business Partners – with your consent or as permitted by law

  • Successors – in connection with a merger, acquisition, or sale of assets

We do not sell your personal information.

4. Your Choices

You may:

  • Opt out of marketing emails
  • Update or correct your Personal Information
  • Delete your account or request data removal

To exercise these choices, contact us at:

📧 hello@lume.com

We implement reasonable security measures to protect your information; however, no transmission over the Internet is 100% secure.

7. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy. The updated version will be posted on the Platform with a revised “Last Updated” date.
