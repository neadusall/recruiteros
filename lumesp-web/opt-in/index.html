About our text messaging program

Lume Search Partners (“Lume,” “we,” or “us”) operates a recruiting text-messaging program. If you provide your mobile number and opt in above, we may send you recurring text messages about your application, job opportunities, interview scheduling, requests for information, and related recruiting updates.

  • Message frequency varies based on your application activity and our communications with you.
  • Message and data rates may apply. Lume does not charge for the messages, but your mobile carrier’s standard rates may apply.
  • Reply STOP to any message to unsubscribe at any time. Reply HELP for help, or email info@lumesp.com.
  • Consent to receive text messages is not a condition of applying for any position or of any employment.
  • Carriers are not liable for delayed or undelivered messages.
  • No mobile information or SMS opt-in consent will be shared with or sold to third parties or affiliates for marketing or promotional purposes.

Full details are in our SMS / Text Messaging Terms and Privacy Policy.
