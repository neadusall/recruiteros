The BD motion

Signal → Account → Buyer → Job order

Business development is the same engine pointed at clients instead of candidates.

📡

Signal

A funding round, a hiring surge, a new VP, an expansion, the system flags companies the instant they're likely to need help hiring.

🏢

Account

Score the company against your ICP and disqualifiers, so you only chase accounts worth your time.

🧭

Buyer

Decision-maker discovery surfaces the exact hiring manager, VP, or founder who signs the order.

🧾

Job order

Signal-based outreach and AMD calls book the discovery call, and the mandate that follows.

Tools in the BD OS

Built for winning clients

💰

Account signals

Fundraising, hiring velocity, leadership changes, expansion, and M&A, the events that mean a company needs to hire now.

🏢

Account targeting & ICP

Define your ideal client once; the system scores and ranks accounts against it automatically.

🧭

Decision-maker discovery

Find and verify the right buyer at each account, the person who actually signs the job order.

⚡

Company enrichment

Headcount, tech stack, funding, growth indicators, and buyer contact info, resolved per account.

✉️

Client outreach

Informed, signal-based sequences that open with what's actually happening at the account, not "are you hiring?"

📞

BD voice dialer + AMD

Dial buyers at scale with Telnyx Premium answering-machine detection, humans connect, machines get a voicemail drop.

🗂️

CRM pipeline

Track every account from queued → discovery booked → mandate signed, synced to your system of record.

📈

Job-order analytics

See which signals and industries convert to mandates, and attribute every new client to its source.
