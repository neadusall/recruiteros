## Operating rules
- 4 weekly waves; the voicemail lands the SAME DAY as that week's email.
- Weekdays only. Each wave varies the day-of-week AND time-of-day vs the prior (e.g. Tue 10:40a → Thu 2:15p → Mon 4:05p → Wed 11:25a, in their local time).
- No frequency cap on these 4 voicemails (cap removed for this sequence).
- No direct phone → skip the voicemail. If they have ACCEPTED the LinkedIn connection → send a LinkedIn voice note instead.
- Length: AMD voicemail 20–25s (~55 words). LinkedIn voice note up to 45s (~100 words).
- Weeks 2–4 voice leads with its OWN value; the email is a light "I also left a note", never "did you get my email".
- Any reply at any point halts everything and sends the booking link.
