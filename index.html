Signals, not spray-and-pray

Signals replace cold outreach

Every campaign starts with a trigger, a funding round, a new VP, a hiring surge. Context creates relevance. Relevance creates response.

✕ Interruption-based

"Hi, are you hiring?"

Send 1,000 messages → hope 10 respond → hope 1 is interested.

✓ Signal-based

"I noticed you've posted three engineering leadership roles in the last 14 days while expanding into healthcare. Most teams hit capacity during that transition, curious how you're handling talent acquisition."

Triggered by: 3 new job posts + territory expansion signal.
