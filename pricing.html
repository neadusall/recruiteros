FAQ

Questions, answered

Do I have to rip out my current ATS?

No. RecruiterOS sits above your ATS and syncs both ways. Loxo, Greenhouse, Lever, Bullhorn, Ashby and more stay your system of record, we just keep them current and de-duped.

Is this for recruiting or business development?

Both. They run on the same infrastructure. The same signal that says a company is hiring creates a placement opportunity and a client opportunity, so every recruiter can also develop business.

What is the answering-machine detection feature?

Our voice dialer uses Telnyx Premium AMD. When it detects a human, the call connects to a recruiter; when it detects a machine, it drops a clean voicemail, transcribes it, and logs the outcome, so you never burn time on a beep.

How do the AI SMS replies work?

The SMS layer understands the campaign, role, and full conversation history. It replies in real time, detects when interest spikes, and routes hot leads to a human. You can switch auto-reply off on any thread to take over.

Can I bring my own provider accounts?

Yes, connect your own domains, mailboxes, LinkedIn seats, and numbers via your API keys. RecruiterOS orchestrates them; you keep ownership and deliverability control.

What happens when I run out of credits?

Nothing breaks, the workspace keeps working. You can top up anytime or upgrade your plan; unused Pro/Team credits roll over for one month.
