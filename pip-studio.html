Recording tips for great lip-sync
  • Start with your mouth closed and neutral, looking at the camera.
  • Even lighting on your face, lips in focus.
  • Hold still for the first second (the name is synced there).
  • Record at 30fps, 720p or better, face filling the frame.
  • Quiet room, decent mic. The same clip also clones your voice.
