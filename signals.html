What we watch

The signals that create opportunity

Each one is a reason to reach out, and the raw material for a relevant first line.

💰

Fundraising

Seed to Series D and beyond. New capital means new headcount, and budget to fill it. Be first in the inbox the week the round is announced.

📈

Hiring velocity

A sudden spike in open roles signals a team stretched past capacity. We track posting cadence per company and flag the surge.

👔

Leadership change

A new VP or CTO rebuilds their org within 90 days. Catch the window when they're hiring their team and choosing vendors.

📉

Layoffs & restructuring

Great talent hits the market in batches. We detect reductions and surface the affected senior people before competitors do.

🌍

Expansion & new markets

New office, new region, new product line, all create greenfield teams that need building from scratch.

🤝

M&A activity

Mergers reshuffle org charts and free up talent. Acquisitions create integration hiring. Both are timing-sensitive openings.

Candidate-side signals

Signals aren't just for accounts. RecruiterOS watches the talent side too, so you reach out when someone is most likely to listen.

  • Promotions passed over and quiet title stagnation
  • Employer distress, layoffs, down rounds, leadership exits

Signal pull → scoring

From noise to a ranked work-list

Raw signals are noisy. RecruiterOS scores them against your ICP so you only act on what matters.

1

Pull

The engine continuously ingests funding databases, job boards, news, and profile changes across your tracked market.

2

Match

Each signal is matched to your Ideal Customer Profile and disqualifiers, wrong size, wrong geo, wrong stage drop out automatically.

3

Score

What remains is ranked by urgency and fit, so the hottest, most timely opportunities rise to the top of your queue.

4

Trigger

High-scoring signals auto-launch a campaign, building the target list and drafting outreach grounded in the signal itself.
