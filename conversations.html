Voice & AMD

AI dialer with answering-machine detection

The phone still closes deals, but only if a human picks up. RecruiterOS dials at scale, knows the instant a machine answers, and never wastes a recruiter on a voicemail beep.

Powered by Telnyx Premium AMD

Talk to humans. Voicemail the rest.

Every outbound call runs Telnyx Premium answering-machine detection. Live humans connect straight to a rep; machines get a clean, pre-recorded voicemail drop, automatically.

  • Human detected → warm-transfer to the recruiter, screen-pop with context
  • Machine detected → drop a tailored voicemail after the beep, log it, move on
  • Every call recorded & transcribed, then summarized by AI
  • Outcomes dispatched to your ATS, no manual call notes
